My group's presentation on the Lorenz Snow System

In Fall 2022, Hayley Coyle, Sebastian Osorio, and I worked together on a project for an course on Applied Dynamical Systems at UC Santa Cruz. The focus of the presentation was on a section of Edward Lorenz's book, "The Essence of Chaos". In his book, Lorenz details and analyzes a 6-variable dynamical system.

Some details of the work we did on this project:

  • Discrete and Continuous Dynamical Systems
  • Analysis of Nullclines and Fixed Points
  • Analysis of Dissipative v.s. Conservative fields
  • MatLab
    • RK4 Numerical Integrator
    • Constructing Poincare and Interative Maps
    • Visualizing 4D-Chaotic Attractor
