REU in Math-Bio / Fluid Dynamics

In the summer of 2023, I participated in Towson University's REU program under the mentorship of Dr. Herve Nganguia Ph.D and Dr. Jia-An along with Kristen Lloyd, Garrett Hauser, Jazmin Sharp, and Samuel Armstrong.

The focus of our research was to validate analytical results on the topic of the propulsion of spherical ciliated organisms through Stokes Flow, Brinkman Flow, and a Shear-Thinning medium through the use of DeepXDE's deep learning python toolbox. The program ran from June 5th, 2023 to July 28th, 2023. Throughout the program, we postered at the NSA's Summer REU conference and plan to discuss our research at the Ohio State's Young Mathematicians Conference in August, the Annual Division of Fluid Dynamics Meeting in November, as well as the 2024 Joint Mathematics Meeting in San Francisco.
